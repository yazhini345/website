At FundEast, we bring the world to your plate. Specializing in a variety of international cuisines, we offer an exquisite dining experience that takes you on a culinary journey around the globe. Our talented chefs curate dishes from diverse cultures, ensuring that every meal is an adventure.

Our Cuisines

International Delights: From the rich, aromatic spices of Indian curries to the delicate flavors of Japanese sushi, the hearty pastas of Italy, the vibrant dishes of Mexican cuisine, and the classic tastes of American comfort food, our menu has something for everyone.

Signature Dishes:

  • Italian: Creamy Fettuccine Alfredo, Margherita Pizza..
  • Japanese: Fresh Sushi Platters, Tempura
  • Mexican: Spicy Tacos, Enchiladas
  • Indian: Butter Chicken, Paneer Tikka
  • American: Juicy Burgers, BBQ Ribs

Our Ambiance

Step into FundEast and experience a welcoming atmosphere that is perfect for any occasion.
